Services

Tailored experiences for your canine companion

Our four-legged friends come in a variety of shapes and sizes, all with different needs and individual personalities. That’s why at The Dog & Boot I like to get to know you and your furry friend, listening to your needs in order to deliver a service that delivers peace of mind & happiness for both pet and owner. To do this I offer a meet & greet before any walk or stay, most importantly it gives your four-legged friend time to settle and to make sure you are both comfortable with me and my home.

Quality time and individual attention for each dog is important, that’s why I aim to keep group waks and boarding to a maximum of 3 dogs at a time depending on size and individual needs of each of your canine companions.
